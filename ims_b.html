SinkLabs Inventory Management (v1)

This is a simple, free inventory system for retail shops that works with Google Drive and Google Sheets. It offers:

  • Web-based interface
  • Barcode field & image support
  • Works entirely on Google Drive (via Apps Script)
  • Easy to set up and free to use
